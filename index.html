We Can’t Help Everyone, But Everyone Can Help Someone.

We exist to serve the disadvantaged people living with HIV/AIDS and fight against poverty in Rwanda. Compelled by love, compassion and commitment we want to bring safety, Self sustainability and greater self-esteem to the most vulnerable.

Its our Firm believe that though we cant help everyone, we can mobilize those who are like minded to join us towards helping someone, Our love for God has taught us how to love and serve one another towards sustainable Community transformation.
